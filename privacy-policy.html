Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

ReadyStack (readystack.ai) is a product of Makesbridge Technology, Inc. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the ReadyStack platform, including any integrations with third-party services such as Google.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a ReadyStack account or use our services, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information — Name, email address, and credentials you provide during registration.
  • Google Account Data — When you connect your Google account via OAuth, we access specific data as authorized by you through the Google consent screen. This includes Google Sheets data, Google Drive file metadata, Google Contacts data, and Gmail send capability necessary to operate the ReadyStack platform.
  • Usage Data — Information about how you interact with ReadyStack, including pages viewed, features used, and engagement signals.
  • Device & Browser Data — IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs collected automatically through standard web technologies.

2. Google API Scopes & Limited Use Disclosure

ReadyStack requests access to the following Google API scopes when you connect your Google account:

  • Google Sheets API (spreadsheets) — Used to read and write data to Google Sheets that serve as your personal data layer within ReadyStack. This includes storing your network relationships, engagement data, and District configuration.
  • Google Drive API (drive.file) — Used to create, access, and manage files that ReadyStack creates or that you explicitly open with ReadyStack. This scope is limited to files created by or opened with the application — ReadyStack does not access your entire Google Drive.
  • Gmail API (gmail.send) — Used to send emails on your behalf, including District participant invitations, newsletters, automated campaign messages, and district updates. ReadyStack can only send emails — it cannot read, modify, or delete any messages in your Gmail account. All mass communications are initiated by you through the ReadyStack platform.
  • Google People API (contacts.readonly) — Used to display your existing Google Contacts within ReadyStack so you can select and import contacts into your District for participant invitations, newsletters, and automated engagement flows. This access is read-only — ReadyStack cannot modify, create, or delete any contacts in your Google account. Imported contacts are stored within your ReadyStack District data layer and governed by this privacy policy.

ReadyStack's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only use Google data for the purposes described in this policy and as necessary to provide and improve ReadyStack's functionality.
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide or improve the service, to comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger or acquisition with adequate data protection.
  • We do not use Google user data to serve advertising.
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless we have your explicit consent, it is necessary for security purposes, to comply with law, or the data is aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the ReadyStack platform.
  • Read and write to your designated Google Sheets to persist your District data, network relationships, and engagement metrics.
  • Create and manage Google Drive files associated with your ReadyStack account.
  • Send emails on your behalf, including District invitations, newsletters, automated campaigns, and district updates, as initiated by you through the platform.
  • Display and import your Google Contacts into ReadyStack for participant invitations, audience building, newsletter distribution, and automated engagement flows.
  • Improve, personalize, and develop new features for the platform.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support.
  • Monitor platform performance and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your ReadyStack account data is stored on secure servers managed by Makesbridge Technology, Inc. Google OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely within our system to maintain your Google connection across sessions.

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure token storage, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or Google data to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers — With trusted vendors who assist in operating our platform, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance — When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfer — In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate data protection safeguards.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Revoke Google Access — You can disconnect your Google account from ReadyStack at any time through your Google Account Permissions page. Upon revocation, ReadyStack will no longer be able to access your Google Sheets or Drive data.
  • Delete Your Account — You may request account deletion by contacting us at privacy@makesbridge.com. Upon deletion, we will remove your account data and destroy any stored OAuth tokens.
  • Access & Correction — You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.
  • Data Portability — Because your data lives in your own Google Sheets, you always retain full ownership and portability of your data.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

ReadyStack uses cookies and similar technologies for authentication, session management, and analytics. You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though some platform features may not function properly without them.

8. Children's Privacy

ReadyStack is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the effective date. Your continued use of ReadyStack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
