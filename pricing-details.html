The Dividend

Revenue Sharing on Hosted Automation Fees

Residents earn a permanent share of the Hosted Automation Fees generated by district usage. Not a referral bonus — a lifetime financial stake.

Role Share How
Residents 40% Share districts — others run hosted automation
District Founders 20% Create a district used to run hosted automation

Revenue sharing applies to Hosted Automation Fees paid by end customers. Makesbridge retains the platform share to operate backend infrastructure, email delivery, data hosting, and automation services.
