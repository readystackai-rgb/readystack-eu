2. Description of Service

ReadyStack is a Market Relationship Management (MRM) platform that enables users to build, manage, and monetize business network relationships through collaborative structures called "Dark Market Districts." The platform includes:

  • Storyteller Mode — for building and visualizing network relationships through a persistent District Map.
  • Architect Mode — for modeling the financial economics of a District, including revenue projections, customer acquisition cost, and time-to-payback calculations.
  • Builder Mode — for configuring and launching operational ReadyStacks with automation, tasks, and agent workflows.
  • Observatory — for monitoring engagement signals, analytics, and performance across all Districts.
  • ReadyStack Studio — the operating container where users run their go-to-market playbooks, manage hosted automation flows, and track consumption maps.
  • Google Sheets and Google Drive integrations for data persistence and user data ownership.

Financial projections, revenue estimates, and performance metrics displayed within ReadyStack — including Architect Mode simulations — are illustrative models and do not constitute financial advice or guarantees of income.

6. Revenue Sharing & Partner Payments

6.1 The Revenue Share Model

ReadyStack operates a revenue sharing model on Hosted Automation Fees generated by your ReadyStack's customers:

  • Organizers (District creators) — receive 60% of Hosted Automation Fees on customers you directly acquired, and 20% on customers acquired by your network partners.
  • Network Partners (those who share your ReadyStack) — receive 40% of Hosted Automation Fees for life on customers they referred.

Revenue sharing percentages apply to the Hosted Automation Fees paid by end customers. Makesbridge retains the remaining platform share to operate backend infrastructure, email delivery, data hosting, and automation services.
