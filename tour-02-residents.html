Review the tasks from the video and assemble a district to deepen the learning and help audiences execute the lesson.

Tasks: Actions + Automations

Actions

1. Identify your "smallest viable audience" by meticulously defining who you seek to serve
2. Develop a "Purple Cow" offering that is truly remarkable and differentiates you
3. Craft compelling stories that resonate deeply with your smallest viable audience
4. Prioritize consistency in your brand's message and delivery
5. Shift focus from chasing broad attention to cultivating deep influence
6. Implement mechanisms to measure genuine engagement and action

Automations

New subscriber joins niche email list
→ Initiate automated email sequence sharing core brand story
Existing customer provides positive review
→ Send automated thank you with shareable link
"Remarkable" content gets high engagement
→ Create lookalike audience and launch targeted ad
Visitor spends 3+ minutes on About page
→ Trigger personalized pop-up or exit-intent offer
